About Real Madrid


Madrid, being the capital of Spain, and known for their all-white uniforms, which led to its nickname “Los Blancos,”
Real Madrid is one of the world's best-known teams.Real Madrid grew out of Football Club Sky,
a team formed in Madrid in 1897. The club was officially founded in 1902 and joined the Royal Spanish Football Federation in 1909.
Real Madrid is recognised as the greatest football club of the 20th century by FIFA and the best European club
of the same era by the IFFHS( International Federation of Football History and Statistics). In 2004, they were awarded the FIFA Centennial Order of Merit.
Their biggest win was on 13 June 1943, Real Madrid beat Barcelona 11-1 at the Chamartín in the second leg of the Copa del Generalísimo semi-finals.
Real Madrid's biggest loss was when they were famously beaten 8-1 by FC Barcelona in a Copa del Rey match on June 13, 1943.
Some fun facts are; Real Madrid is one of only three clubs in Spain's history that has never been relegated from La Liga's top flight.
Real Madrid is the only club ever to win La Liga five times in succession. It accomplished this feat on two different occasions, the first between 1961 and 1965, and the second between 1986 and 1990.
